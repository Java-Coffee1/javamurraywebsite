## Project Overview
This project started when Dr. Winter from Quantum Technology approached me with a design challenge: how would you suspend a hydrogen tank inside a vacuum chamber?

I was then asked to develop a CAD model of the concept we discussed so it could be included in a project proposal.


### The Idea 
The concept was to use Kevlar cables to suspend the tank inside the chamber. This would allow the position of the tank to be adjusted by tightening or loosening the cables, enabling fine alignment and controlled positioning within the system.

### Conclusion

This project translated an early-stage concept into a workable CAD design suitable for proposal development.

Through this process, I developed stronger skills in concept interpretation, mechanical layout design, and CAD modeling for engineering proposals. I also gained my first glimpse into working with a real client rather than just project-based work.
