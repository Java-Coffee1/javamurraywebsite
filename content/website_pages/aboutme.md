Hello I am Java, I am a high school student who likes to make things.
I have a love for design and prototyping, especially when it comes to turning ideas into real, working systems.

I enjoy programming and building projects in areas like robotics, electronics, and mechatronics. I also spend a lot of time working on 3D printing and mechanical design, including mechanisms and linkages, and I like exploring how physical systems move and interact.

Outside of hardware, I work with software and infrastructure too. I run and managing a homelab, I also experiment with tools like Docker, Proxmox, and self-hosted services to understand how larger systems are built and managed.
